EARTH'S LAST DEFENSE

A massive asteroid field is headed straight for Earth! You're piloting humanity's last hope - a prototype ship equipped with an experimental Resonance Cannon. The cannon is incredibly powerful, but there's a catch: it can only fire when synchronized with your ship's power core rhythm. Listen for the steady pulse - that's your core charging. Fire ON THE BEAT and unleash devastating shots. Fire off-beat and the unstable energy will backfire, damaging your own systems!

Your Mission

Destroy every asteroid before they reach Earth!

Controls

  • WASD: Maneuver your ship
  • Mouse: Aim
  • Left Click: Fire (only on the beat!)

Remember:

  • Listen to the metronome pulse
  • Collect health packs from debris
  • Off-beat shots hurt YOU

Tip: the easiest way to survive is to keep shooting every beat, even if there is nothing there just to keep the rythm!
